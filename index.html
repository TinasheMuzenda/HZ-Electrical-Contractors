Everyday Power Solutions

About HZ Electrical Contractors is a provider of everyday electrical engineering and solar services intended to serve commercial, industrial and domestic needs . The company provides electrical design, installation,supply, maintenance,modifications ,commissioning, repair services and certification(Certificate of Compliance) for the various economic sectors.

That includes real estate, manufacturing, mining industry, agriculture, tourism and municipalities within the Southern African region. Whether you need a simple repair or a complex electrical project, our team of skilled professionals is here to keep your power on.
